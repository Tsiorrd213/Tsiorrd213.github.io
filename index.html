The Game Plan

The AICA Playbook

A step-by-step career accelerator built to take you from generic applicant to hired professional.

01 Spot the Signals
02 Make Contact
03 Show, Don't Tell
04 Ace the Interview
05 Tell Your Story
Phase 01

Spot the Signals

Find high-value opportunities before they ever hit job boards. Learn to map stealth hiring initiatives and use AI to trace unadvertised expansions and project teams.

Key Outcomes:
  • Mapping stealth job openings
  • Smart AI search string hacks
  • Spotting stealth hiring triggers
Phase 02

Make Contact

Skip the application portals and connect directly with hiring managers. Learn to write highly personalized, AI-assisted outreach messages that get replies.

Key Outcomes:
  • Finding direct recruiter emails
  • Hyper-customized DM outreach
  • High-conversion networking flow
Phase 03

Show, Don't Tell

Don't just say you can do the work—prove it. Build quick, AI-boosted micro-projects and case studies that demonstrate immediate value before they even call you.

Key Outcomes:
  • Rapid prototype generation
  • Company-specific value decks
  • AI-enhanced project showreels
Phase 04

Ace the Interview

Train custom AI interview bots to mock-interview you, drilling you on tough team-specific questions and polishing your answers on the fly.

Key Outcomes:
  • Personalized mock simulators
  • Dynamic storytelling loops
  • Real-time communication audits
Phase 05

Tell Your Story

Shape the narrative of your career. Weave your background, skills, and projects into a personal pitch that recruiters won't forget.

Key Outcomes:
  • Authentic, high-impact storytelling
  • Structured personal branding
  • Pitch optimization with AI coaches
